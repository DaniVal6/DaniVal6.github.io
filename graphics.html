Evaluation of web graphics

site evaluated: Apple

Purpose of graphics on this site:

The graphics on Apple have great quality. The banner is one of the great image, which includes the logo of apple with the products that they are selling to their costumers. The big graphic of the webiste on the center is the main thing that they want to sell. It has the most attention and communication. The graphics look good on the white background.

Guildlines for the effective use of graphics

  1. Yes- Graphics fit on the puropose, organization, and the style of the page.
  2. Yes- Large graphics have been avoided.
  3. Yes- Graphics help to guide the viewers' focus to the important content on the page.
  4. Yes- Where text appears in graphics (the banner) there is ample contrast.
  5. Yes- Overly bright and potentially "obnoxious" images have been avoided.
  6. Yes- Graphics have not been used for textual content, other than the banner.
  7. Yes- Alernate text is present for all images.
